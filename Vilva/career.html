Career at VilvaTech


Roles and Responsibilities



Technical Sales Engineer




No of vacancies: 02

Qualifications: Engineers / MBA / Any Graduates or Post Graduates.

Employment Type: Full Time

Experience: 0-2 Years (*Freshers also can apply)

Job Location: Thanjavur and Trichy



  • Chasing Business Targets for the Area
  • Calling and visiting customers – making technical presentations, Demonstrating solutions, and building relationship
  • Planning daily sales schedules and systematically achieving KRA’s of Sales Targets, Payment Recovery, New Business Acquisition, and Customer Satisfaction milestones
  • Managing or coordinating with the Technical team to resolve Customer queries, demands, urgencies, and preferences both technical or procedural.
  • Monitor and timely reporting of competitor
  • Supporting Solution, Technical, and procurement teams to ensure the product/solutions are delivered to customers without any issues.

  • Daily reporting and sales CRM up-dating
  • Being part of reviews and sales-related discussions
  • Timely coordination with the Solution team for estimates and etc
  • Following internal policies and processes in a timely manner-expense reimbursement, discipline, etc

  • Supporting the team in the zone- be it technical/ price/ data, etc
  • Maintaining zero communication breakdowns with other team members
  • Provide technical assistance and problem-solving for the team related to product/solutions sales.
  • Maintain a good teamwork spirit to help others in the team, and develop rapport and relationships with teams in other regions in the zone.

  • Attending skill-building workshops as mandated by the organization and achieving expected scores

    • Drive for results
    • Quick learner
    • Persuasion Skills
    • Confidence
    • Interpersonal Skills
    • Self-motivated
